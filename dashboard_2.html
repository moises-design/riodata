Own the data layer for this region.
RioData is more than a platform — it's a structured, verified, real-time dataset on the most strategically important economic corridor in North America. We're open to licensing, acquisition, or institutional partnership.
What's In the Dataset
🏢
Business Directory
340+ verified companies with sector, services, certifications, capacity, location, contact, and ready-to-work status. Growing weekly.
👷
Workforce Intelligence
Skills gap data by occupation. School-to-employer pipeline. Certification rates. Job opening counts. Wage levels by county and sector.
🏗️
Project Pipeline
Active construction, energy, and infrastructure projects with value, location, timeline, and subcontractor matching data.
📊
Economic Indicators
GDP, employment, wages, business formations, and cross-border trade data aggregated by county and sector — updated monthly.
🎓
Education Pipeline
16 institutions tracked. Program-level grad counts, employment rates, salary outcomes, and employer connection data.
🌐
Cross-Border Coverage
The only platform with structured data on BOTH sides of the border — South Texas counties AND Tamaulipas, Nuevo León, Coahuila.
